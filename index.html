PROJECTS

Arduino Creature

An interactive robotic creature built with Arduino that responds to its environment through sensors and actuators. The project explores basic robotics principles and creates an engaging physical computing experience that mimics lifelike behaviors through simple electronics and code.

Cardboard controller

A high-fidelity physical prototype of a game controller, constructed entirely from recycled cardboard. This project investigates the limits of rigid materials in replicating complex, organic curves and ergonomic forms. By utilizing advanced scoring and structural layering techniques, the model demonstrates how low-fidelity materials can be used for sophisticated industrial design explorations.

Motion Conversion Study

The core objective of this project was to master the transition from a digital 3D model to a high-functioning physical mechanism. The challenge centered on the classic mechanical problem of converting circular motion into linear motion, requiring strict adherence to tolerances and material properties.
